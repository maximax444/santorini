Important notes

Due to the specificity of the island, it won't be possible to shoot groups bigger than 3-5 persons at one time since walking in the tiny streets of Santorini with such a big group is very uncomfortable for the group itself and for people trying to have rest in a quiet area of the hotels around. For this reason, a kind of schedule for the shoot has to be made: the entire group must be divided into several parts of 3-5 people. After one part has finished the shoot another part arrives. If you want a picture of the whole group that will be possible at the very end of the photoshoot or at the very beginning of it

In order to save time for the group if it's limited, you can order 2 or 3 photoshoots which can be held at the same time parallelly with different photographers. In this case, the price of each shoot will be counted as a separate shoot
